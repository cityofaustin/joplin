Procedure

TO PREP: Combine olive oil, garlic, orange and lime juices, cumin, oregano, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper in a large measuring cup or cambro and blend until smooth with stick blender. Pour HALF of marinade over chicken and toss well to coat. Marinate chicken for 1-2 hours. Reserve remaining half of marinade.

While chicken is marinating, toss potatoes with .25 cup olive oil, 1 tbl salt, and 1 tbl pepper and roast in 350 degree oven until tender and beginning to brown. Cool. Bring remaining marinade to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes. Set aside to cool.

Remove chicken from marinade and arrange on sheet pans (discard any marinade leftover from the chicken). Bake at 350 until chicken temps at 165. Cool, then chop into bite-sized pieces. Combine chicken, potatoes, and sauce and mix well.
